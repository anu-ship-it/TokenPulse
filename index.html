Features

Everything you need to
stay ahead of the limit.

Built for anyone who writes long documents, runs deep research sessions, or just has long conversations across AI platforms.

📊
Live Token Bar
A slim bar above every input box shows how much context you have left — updates with every message.
All 4 platforms
⚡
Real Rate Limit Data
Pulls your actual 5-hour session and 7-day weekly usage from Claude's internal API. Exact numbers, exact reset times.
Claude only

Smart Notifications
Alerts at 75%, 90%, and 100% — threshold-based not timer-based. Fires once per crossing, resets when usage drops.
All 4 platforms
